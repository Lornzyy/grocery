About Us

Our founder Lorna Mungai had a dream for Kenya of having fresh and affordable groceries in the urban places since Kenyans believed that for the groceries to be the best they had to go to the markets.
Fresh - Hub Grocery aims to bring you the freshest and high quality groceries at affordable prices and with conviniency with our various shops around Nairobi.
As a company we aim to support our country by ensuring all the products we have are Kenyan sourced from Kenyan farmers and also from our company farms.
